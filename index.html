RENDEZVOUS PRESSURE AND SOFT WASHING CO.

Fresno, California

Rendezvous offers a multitude of cleaning services which include:
  • Concrete Cleaning
  • House Cleaning
  • Deck Cleaning
  • Gutter Cleaning
One of the services we have is concrete cleaning. This includes driveways, pathways, and any other concrete service you have on your property. We have a combination of pressure washers to provide great results on your concrete areas.
Rendezvous provides soft washing for housing as well to ensure that materials are not damaged. However, if you would rather do a simple pressure wash to your house we can still accommodate your wishes. Pressure washing will still leave your house clean but we do recommend using soft washing on homes to prevent damages and ensure longer lasting results.
Got a dirty deck that needs cleaning? Not a problem! Our company also offers deck cleaning services to our customers to help get those decks looking vibrant. Our solutions help get all the organic matter that build up easily in these wood surfaces.
Rendezvous Pressure and Soft Washing Co. provides gutter cleaning service for your home as well. We have the tools not only clear out all the junk and residue that lies within the gutters, we also have tools and solutions that help clean the outside of your gutters. Our service will leave your gutters looking beautiful inside and out!
