My name is Juan Robles and I started Rendezvous Pressure and Soft Washing Co. three years ago in my hometown of Fresno, California. It all started with a van full of simple supplies to pressure the homes of my city but has turned into the sophisicated services that we provide to all people of Central Valley, California. Being in this business for three years has allowed us gain a wealth of knowledge on how to go about our job to ensure that you receive the best and safest service there is. Although we made our start just by cleaning driveways and rinsing off houses, we have transformed into concrete cleaning, house cleaning, deck cleaning, and gutter cleaning. Our business has learned to use proven supplies and solutions to make sure that your home looks brand new by the end of it.
