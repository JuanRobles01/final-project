My name is Juan Robles and I started Rendezvous Pressure and Soft Washing Co. three years ago in my hometown of Fresno, California. It all started with a van full of simple supplies to pressure wash the homes of my city but has turned into the sophisicated services that we provide to all people of Central Valley, California. Being in this business for three years has allowed us gain a wealth of knowledge on how to go about our job to ensure that you receive the best and safest service there is. Although we made our start just by cleaning driveways and rinsing off houses, we have transformed into concrete cleaning, house cleaning, deck cleaning, and gutter cleaning. Our business has learned to use proven supplies and solutions to make sure that your home looks brand amazing by the end of it. Our services currently reach out to the entire Central Valley but we are on a mission to be able to provide our work to the whole state of California. Thank you for reading about our business and we hope to see you soon!
